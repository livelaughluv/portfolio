Barbershop Services + Improvements

The project for this quarter was to research common services and how they organize their data. My group (Thomas, Helen and me) chose to focus on barbershops and the services they provide, as well as the issues with their data. We decided it is difficult to capitalize on cutting other people’s hair and data is often kept physically in logbooks which is potentially dangerous, and that capitalizing on a target audience and selling products specified for them and market better, also keeping records digitally to further protect information and ensure data security

Documentation Issues: Many barber shops have issues in documentation regarding which barber served which customers and when; this could be solved with digital records and cloud backups to prevent the data loss common with physical recordings.

Training Issues: Many barbers aren’t given many formal classes; their main focus is actual practice, intern work. That being said, many barbers are mistreated, overworked, and taught bad practices; this could be solved by having sanctioned, monitored internships at reputable shops rather than any shop of choice that may promote bad practices prior to certification.

Company Data: Company data should have cloud backups and hard drive backups after passing periods or major events so that one is presumably always intact, functional, and up to date. This will help prevent any possible loss of transaction history, the client’s name and their barber, and other necessary information to record for safety or functional purposes.

Client Data: Little client data is collected at barber shops aside from possibly phone numbers and payment information, a simple way of protecting this data is not saving said information as it is unnecessary to function as a business.

Protect and Maintain the Company: Control access of data within the company to protect against any possible breaches in security while also backing up data to maintain data.

Protect and Maintain the Client: Create a data usage policy to protect clients information from being misused in any way while the business has it in possession and controlling access of data to further protect said data while also limiting possible any unrecorded changes.
